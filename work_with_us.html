Work with us

Join ARM-lab at the Technical University of Košice


We welcome motivated Postdoctoral researchers, PhD candidates, and MSc students who want to collaborate with us on continuum, snake and soft robotics, and AI-based planning & control.

What we offer

  • Supervision and collaboration with experienced researchers in robotics and AI
  • Access to our prototyping & experimental setups (continuum/soft robots)
  • International publications and conference participation
  • Support with MSCA PF, National Scholarship Programme (NSP), Erasmus+ traineeships, DAAD RISE
  • Cooperation with industrial partners on real-world problems

How to join

Postdocs

We host researchers through MSCA Postdoctoral Fellowships and other mobility schemes. Send us your topic proposal and CV.

PhD candidates

Possible via the National Scholarship Programme (NSP), the Visegrad Fund, or through industrial mini-projects (industrial PhD).

MSc students

We welcome 2–6 month Erasmus+ internships; in summer also DAAD RISE Worldwide (for German students).
